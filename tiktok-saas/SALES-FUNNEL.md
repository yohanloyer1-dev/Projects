| 1 | What's your name? | Short text | Yes |

| 6 | Why now? What changed that makes this worth doing in the next 90 days? | Long text | Yes |
| 7 | What's your monthly budget for this kind of system? | Multiple choice: <€500 / €500-1000 / €1000-2000 / €2000+ | Yes |
| 8 | What's your timeline? | Multiple choice: This week / This month / Next 90 days / Just exploring | Yes |
| 9 | What's the biggest blocker stopping you from doing TikTok yourself today? | Long text | Yes |
| 10 | Are you OK with an AI character on screen (not your real face)? | Yes/No | Yes |
| 11 | Anything else I should know? | Long text | No |
| 12 | Where did you hear about this? | Multiple choice: LinkedIn / Email / Referral / Other | Yes |
