# Rice Cooker Benchmark Project

## User Profile
- **Name:** Yohan
- **Location:** Greece (travels between Athens and Nice)
- **Family:** 3 people (including toddler born Nov 2024)
- **Background:** E-commerce, data-driven approach
- **Communication:** Prefers clear explanations, no shortcuts

## Project Goal
Find the best rice cooker available in Greece that meets strict health/safety criteria.

## Critical Requirements (Deal-Breakers)

### 1. Safe Materials (TOP PRIORITY)
**AVOID:**
- PTFE (Teflon)
- PFOA/PFAS
- Bare aluminum
- Cheap non-stick that peels

**PREFER:**
- Certified ceramic (BPA/PTFE/PFOA/PFAS-free)
- Stainless steel 304/316
- Quality non-stick with TUV/CE certs

### 2. Keep-Warm Function
- Must keep rice warm 6-12+ hours
- Essential for flexibility with young child

### 3. Available in Greece
- Skroutz.gr (main marketplace)
- Greek retailers: Kotsovolos, Plaisio, MediaMarkt, Public
- EU Amazon (DE, FR, IT) with Greece shipping
- Yum-Asia.com/eu (ships from Amsterdam)

### 4. Capacity
- Minimum 1.5L (ideal: 1.8-2L)
- Family of 3 + meal prep

### 5. Budget
- Target: 50-100 EUR
- Flexible up to 150-200 EUR if justified

## Products Already Eliminated
- Cecotec RiceFusion 7000 (burns rice)
- Princess 271940/271950 (breaks quickly)
- Xiaomi Mi Smart (Chinese app required)
- Tefal budget models (coating issues)
- Various small models <1.5L

## Promising Products Identified
- Yum Asia Kumo (~112 EUR with shipping) - ceramic, fuzzy logic
- Hendi 240410 (~56 EUR) - HoReCa brand, good reviews
- Sencor SRM series (~70-82 EUR) - Czech brand

## Special Mission
Find Japanese (Zojirushi, Tiger) or Korean (Cuckoo) brands available with EU voltage (220-240V) and reasonable shipping to Greece.

## Output Files
- data/products.csv - All products found
- data/discovered_retailers.csv - Shops found
- reports/analysis_report.md - Final recommendations
