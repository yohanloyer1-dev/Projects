## Health & Safety Summary

### Material Safety Ranking

| Rank | Material | Safety | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|-------|
| **1st** | Stainless Steel (uncoated) | ✅ SAFEST | No coatings, decades of research, lasts forever |
| **2nd** | Certified Ceramic | ✅ SAFE | PFAS-free, choose reputable brands with certifications |
| **3rd** | Standard Non-stick/Teflon | ❌ AVOID | PFAS concerns, coating degrades, potential leaching |

### Why Stainless Steel Wins for Health
- No coatings that can flake or degrade
- Minimal leaching (only with acidic foods cooked for hours - not rice)
- Decades of safety research
- Lasts a lifetime

### Why Ceramic is Second Best
- PFAS/PTFE-free
- Generally safe according to toxicologists
- Some concerns about titanium dioxide nanoparticles in cheaper brands
- Quality brands with BPA/PTFE/PFAS certifications (like Yum Asia) are much safer
- Shorter lifespan (2-5 years)
