🔬 Material Safety: Ceramic vs Stainless Steel

Factor Stainless Steel Certified Ceramic
Overall Safety ✅ SAFEST - No coatings at all ✅ SAFE - PFAS-free when certified
Coating concerns ✅ None - nothing to flake off ⚠️ Can wear after 2-5 years
Chemical leaching ⚠️ Minimal nickel (only with acidic foods) ✅ None when intact
Rice sticking ❌ Rice can stick more ✅ Non-stick surface
Durability ✅ Lasts decades ⚠️ 2-5 years typical
Research available ✅ Decades of studies ⚠️ Newer, less long-term data

Bottom Line

Stainless steel is technically safest (no coatings), but certified ceramic from reputable brands is also very safe and makes better rice (non-stick). For rice cooking specifically, certified ceramic is the practical choice.
