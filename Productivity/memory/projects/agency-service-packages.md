## Positioning

**Agency promise:** Turn Gorgias from a support cost into a revenue channel.
**Not:** IT services, dev agency, generic CX consulting.
**Yes:** Productized, outcome-oriented, Gorgias-only, premium.

Pricing philosophy: Value-based, not hourly. Every package has a clear outcome, not a deliverable list. The client buys the result, not your time.

---

## Package Architecture

Three tiers + a retainer. Simple, clear, no custom quoting on first contact.

---

## Package 1: AUDIT
**Tagline:** "Know exactly what's broken and what to fix first."

**Who it's for:** Gorgias users who feel stuck — using the tool but not seeing results. They know something's off but don't know where to start.

**Scope:**
- Full Gorgias account audit (views, macros, rules, AI Agent, integrations, team setup)
- Customer-facing CX audit (response time, CSAT baseline, top ticket categories)
- Automation rate + coverage rate analysis
- 3-page prioritised action report: what to fix in 30 / 60 / 90 days
- 45-min debrief call

**Deliverable:** Prioritised CX Roadmap document (PDF)

**Price:** €1,500 — €2,500 (depending on brand size / complexity)

**Timeline:** 5 business days

**Ohtani check:** This is a foot-in-the-door product. Low friction, high trust-building. Most audits lead to Setup or Retainer.

---

## Package 2: SETUP
**Tagline:** "Your Gorgias, fully configured to work without you."

**Who it's for:** New Gorgias users, or brands that bought Gorgias but never configured it properly. Also: brands who just got the Audit and want it fixed.

**Scope:**
- Everything from Audit (if not done yet)
- Full Gorgias configuration: views, macros, rules, tags, routing, team structure
- AI Agent setup: guidances for top 10 intents, handover rules, tone calibration
- WISMO automation (if ShipStation/ShipBob connected)
- Returns flow (if Loop/Returnly connected)
- Integration setup (Shopify, ShipStation, up to 2 integrations)
- Team training (1x async Loom walkthrough per role)
- 30-day check-in call

**Deliverable:** Live configured Gorgias account + documentation

**Price:** €3,500 — €6,000 (small-medium DTC) / €6,000 — €10,000 (multi-brand/enterprise)

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks

**Upsell:** → Retainer (maintain + optimise ongoing)

---

## Package 3: AI AGENT SPRINT
**Tagline:** "0% to 40%+ automation in 30 days."

**Who it's for:** Brands with Gorgias + AI Agent who are stuck below 20% automation. They know the potential but can't unlock it.

**Scope:**
- AI Agent deep audit (coverage rate, success rate, handover reasons, ignored tickets)
- Phase 1 quick wins: handover rules, top 10 guidances, tone fixes
- Phase 2 structural: Order Management activation, Baback/returns integration
- Chat widget deployment (if not live)
- Weekly check-ins during 30-day sprint
- Final report: before/after metrics

**Deliverable:** Measurable automation rate improvement (target: +30-50 percentage points)

**Price:** €4,000 — €7,500

**Timeline:** 30 days

**Note:** This is the Nébuleuse Bijoux playbook, productized. You've already proven it works.

## Package 4: RETAINER — CX OPS
**Tagline:** "Your CX keeps improving every month. On autopilot."

**Who it's for:** Post-setup clients. Brands growing fast who need ongoing Gorgias optimization without hiring a CX specialist.

**Scope (monthly):**
- Monthly Gorgias health check (automation rate, CSAT, response time, SLA compliance)
- 1 optimisation sprint per month (new guidance, new rule, new flow, or integration fix)
- Priority Slack/email support (48h response)
- Quarterly strategy review call

**Price:** €600 — €1,500/month (depending on brand complexity)

**Minimum commitment:** 3 months

**Anchor:** Accessory Partners is at ~€750/mo. This is the proof point.

## Package 5: AGENCY ENABLEMENT
**Tagline:** "Make your team Gorgias-certified. Fast."

**Who it's for:** Shopify/eComm agencies that want to add Gorgias as a service offering. Your referral network. Yohan's existing partner base at Gorgias.

**Scope:**
- Half-day or full-day team training (async Loom library or live workshop)
- Gorgias Playbook PDF (your proprietary methodology)
- AI Agent quickstart guide + prompt templates
- Discovery questionnaire for their clients
- Ongoing Q&A access (Slack, 60 days)

**Price:** €2,500 (async package) / €5,000 (live workshop)

**Note:** This is the B2B2C play. Each agency you train becomes a referral source.

## Pricing Principles

1. **Never quote hourly.** Always scope and price as a package.
2. **Audit price anchors Setup.** A €2K audit makes €5K setup feel reasonable.
3. **Retainer is the goal.** Every project should be pitched with a retainer path.
4. **Price for outcomes, not deliverables.** "40% automation in 30 days" is worth more than "10 guidances configured."
5. **Price anchors up, not down.** Start at the top of the range. Discount only for long commitments.

---

## What NOT to Offer (at launch)

- Custom development work
- Platforms other than Gorgias
- Hourly consulting
- Social media management
- General "CX strategy" without Gorgias implementation

*Constraint keeps positioning sharp and pricing premium.*
