## High-Leverage Optimisations by Category

### 1. Partner Follow-ups & Pipeline Tracking

**Problem:** Partner follow-ups are ad hoc. No system for tracking partner-sourced vs partner-influenced attribution.

**Opportunities:**
- **Granola → Claude pipeline:** Pull meeting notes from Granola, extract commitments, surface next actions automatically. *Task already in dashboard.*
- **HubSpot MCP:** You have this connected. Use it to query partner pipeline data, identify stale deals, flag at-risk accounts — in seconds, not 30 minutes in the CRM.
- **Weekly Partner Pulse (template):** A standard 5-item weekly check-in template for each key partner. Claude generates the draft from Granola notes + HubSpot data. You edit + send in <10 min.

### 2. Partner Enablement

**Problem:** Enablement is time-intensive to build and hard to scale. Custom for each partner = not scalable.

**Opportunities:**
- **Gorgias Help Center docs (already building):** 61 articles → 100+ becomes an enablement library for every agency partner you work with. This is a direct asset for both the Agency and the Gorgias role.
- **Quick-win playbooks per partner type:** 3-5 page PDFs for different partner profiles (pure agencies, hybrid, tech partners). Claude builds, you approve. Send once, used forever.
- **Enablement Notion template:** Standardised partner onboarding + enablement space you can duplicate for each new partner. Setup once, clone forever.

### 3. Internal Reporting

**Problem:** Reporting to leadership takes time and feels like theater.

**Opportunities:**
- **Partner-sourced ARR tracker:** Simple spreadsheet connected to HubSpot data. Claude pulls the summary weekly: "Here's where partner-sourced ARR stands, what moved, what's stale."
- **Monthly partner activity report template:** Claude drafts from your notes + HubSpot. You spend 10 min reviewing vs 2h building.
- **Pipeline narrative framing:** When presenting partner pipeline to leadership, Claude can help you frame wins compellingly and anticipate questions.

### 4. Events & Keynotes

**Problem:** Event prep (talk prep, briefing docs, follow-up plans) takes disproportionate time.

**Opportunities:**
- **Talk template system:** Reusable structure for keynotes/webinars. Opening hook → 3 insights → CTA. Claude builds first draft from your bullet points.
- **Post-event follow-up automation:** After each event, a standard follow-up sequence. Segmented by: met at event / watched online / just discovered you. Claude drafts, you approve.
- **Event brief template:** One-page doc for every event you attend: who'll be there, goals, talking points, intro lines, follow-up plan. Claude generates from calendar + context.

### 5. New Partner Biz Dev

**Problem:** Research and outreach for new agency partners is slow and inconsistent.

**Opportunities:**
- **Agency research brief (Claude-generated):** Before any new partner conversation — Claude pulls website, LinkedIn, estimated Shopify stack, and gives you a 1-page brief. 10 minutes → 10 seconds.
- **Personalised outreach template system:** 3 outreach types (cold, warm intro, post-event). Claude adapts the core template per agency. You review + send.
- **EMEA partner map:** A living document of EMEA agencies by tier, region, and partnership status. Claude helps maintain and surface gaps.
