## Content Strategy

**Cadence:** 2 posts/week | **Language:** English only
**Best times:** Tuesday or Thursday, 8–10am local time

### 5 Content Pillars
1. CX as a Revenue Lever
2. Gorgias Playbooks & Tactical Tips
3. CS Automation & Efficiency
4. Retention, Loyalty & CX Psychology
5. Agency / Operator Real Talk

### Top Hook Formulas
1. Problem + Data: "Try X. Most brands never do. Here's why: [stat]"
2. Contrarian: "Most brands don't have X problem. They have Y problem."
3. Story Setup: "Had a call last week with an agency... I noticed something."
4. Insider Claim: "I've never seen a brand with [X] also have [Y]. Not once."
5. Direct Challenge: "Stop doing X. Here's what to do instead."

### Engagement Rules
- Comment on 3–5 target audience posts BEFORE publishing (warms algorithm)
- Reply to every comment in first 2 hours (resets distribution window)
- Hooks never start with "I"
