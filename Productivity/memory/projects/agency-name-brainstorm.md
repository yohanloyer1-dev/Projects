## Criteria (derived from agency identity)

A great name for this agency must:
- Sound premium and credible to both US and European e-commerce brands
- Be pronounceable in EN / FR / ES without awkwardness
- Suggest CX expertise, revenue impact, or intelligent automation — not generic IT services
- Not literally say "Gorgias" (you can't own that trademark)
- Be available as a .com domain (check before committing)
- Be short — 1-2 words max, ideally under 12 characters
- Have no obvious negative connotations in FR or ES

## Naming Patterns to Avoid

- Anything with "CX", "Gorgias", "Help", "Support", "Desk" — too generic
- Anything ending in "-ly" or "-ify" — oversaturated
- Names that are hard to spell after hearing them once
- Names that read as French-only (limits US perception)
