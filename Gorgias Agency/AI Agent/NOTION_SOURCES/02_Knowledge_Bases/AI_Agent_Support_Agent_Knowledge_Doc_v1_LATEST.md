# <span color="purple_bg">🤖</span><span color="purple_bg" discussion-urls="{{discussion://d266382e-bcea-48c7-be78-0aaf59a9ba3a}}"> How does AI Agent (Support) work</span> {toggle="true"}
	AI Agent is an AI-powered support automation tool that enables ecommerce brands to resolve up to 60% of support tickets across email, contact forms, and chat. It connects directly to your Help Center, Shopify store, third-party apps, and internal documentation like Guidance and external resources.
	When a customer reaches out, AI Agent identifies the intent behind the message, pulls relevant order or account information, and either responds with a personalized, on-brand reply or fully resolves the issue using built-in Actions, such as canceling an order, updating a shipping address, or managing subscriptions.
	If more context is needed or confidence is low, AI Agent seamlessly hands the ticket off to your support team. All responses go through an internal QA check, and teams can review and give feedback to help AI Agent improve over time.
	Guidance determines how AI Agent should respond; Actions define what it can do. When both are present, Guidance takes priority. AI Agent is quick to set up, fully customizable, and built to reduce ticket volume, boost CSAT, and free up your team for more complex support work.

	### AI Agent Reasoning
	**Understand exactly why your AI Agent made a decision—and how to improve automation.**
	Until now, AI Agent has operated as a powerful but opaque system: it could respond, hand over, close, or take actions, but you couldn’t see *why*. That’s changing.
	With **AI Agent Reasoning**, every decision your AI Agent makes comes with a clear, transparent explanation.
	### 💡 What It Does
	Every time AI Agent sends a message, performs an action, closes a ticket, or escalates to your team, it now includes a **"Show Reasoning"** panel in the ticket view. This includes:
	1. **A concise explanation** of why the decision was made
	2. **References to specific knowledge sources** used—like guidance, Help Center articles, or product data
	3. **Clickable links to those sources** so you can audit or update them as needed
	4. **Consistent formatting** across outcomes (send, close, handover, wait) for easier review and pattern recognition
	### 🙋 Why It Matters
	Merchants often struggle with the "black box" nature of AI. Why did it choose that wording? Why didn’t it respond? Why did it escalate?
	AI Agent Reasoning gives you **answers**, which unlock three key benefits:
	- **Increased trust:** Understand how the AI thinks and when it's safe to let go of manual reviews
	- **Faster debugging:** Spot errors in knowledge or logic without guessing
	- **Smarter optimization:** See which articles or policies the AI relies on—and fix only what matters
	This closes the feedback loop between AI decisions and your support content. No more blind tweaks to your Help Center and Guidance—just focused, high-impact edits that directly improve automation.

## AI Agent for Contact Form {toggle="true"}
	- AI Agent can now answer messages that come in via contact forms (and will by default do so if the connected email address of the contact form is also connected to AI Agent!)
	- The Ticket Subject will be the same for Contact Form messages - do note that ticket subjects are different between Help Center and Standalone Contact Forms and adapt guidance accordingly if needed), here are some executions of this guidance in action ([#1]({{https://artemisathletix.gorgias.com/app/views/254343/464541354}}), [#2]({{https://artemisathletix.gorgias.com/app/views/254343/464541316}}), [#3]({{https://artemisathletix.gorgias.com/app/views/254343/464540926}}))
		- You can use the same technique (referencing the ticket subject) to achieve tagging, check out the configuration on [artemis]({{https://artemisathletix.gorgias.com/app/automation/shopify/artemisathletix/ai-agent}}) for an example
	- You can use rules to add `ai_ignore` tags to not answer to contact form messages (multiple options here, ticket subject, `via`, ...)
	- 👍 Actions can be executed via contact form, which inherently makes sense as long as the email correlated to contact form is the same as the email for AI Agent.
	::: callout {icon="📝" color="gray_bg"}
	No Front-end changes, everything works via the connected emails and exactly the same as email
	:::

	## FAQs {toggle="true"}
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: Does AI Agent support **<span discussion-urls="{{discussion://2161ae21-78f5-8040-9ee6-001c5ce3e16e}}">**images**</span>**?**</summary>
			Yes, AI Agent will support images as of <mention-date start="2025-07-16"/> , and that unlocks major automation gains.
			Customers often include images in support requests—whether it’s a photo of a damaged item, a screenshot of an issue, or proof of return. AI Agent is designed to handle these visual inputs intelligently. It can **receive, interpret, and take action on image attachments**, allowing brands to automate even more complex support scenarios that previously required manual review.
			This capability is especially valuable in high-volume workflows like:
			- **Damage claims**
			- **Order issues**
			- **Missing or incorrect items**
			- **Return and refund requests**
			By enabling AI Agent to process images alongside text, merchants can unlock an additional layer of automation—often increasing automation rates by 10–15% in ticket types that typically required human eyes.
			And when paired with Actions, AI Agent doesn’t just understand the image, it can take the next step, like initiating a replacement order or issuing a refund, without agent intervention.
			Ref: <mention-page url="{{https://www.notion.so/1581ae2178f58077982dc9dbfea56488}}"/> 
		</details>
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: Can AI Agent calculate time between a shoppers message and when an order was placed?**</summary>
			Yes, we can now pre-calculate the time between a shopper message and order placed and passing this information to AI Agent. 
		</details>
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: Is order data still only available for the last 5 orders created in the last 120 days?** </summary>
			- Ever had AI Agent tell a shopper with a three year old order that their product is probably still within the 1-year-warranty? This sometimes happened because we had a hard limit of 120 days of order history we would pass to AI Agent. 
				- We removed the 120 day limit and in test cases (we tested especially warranty questions like the one I mentioned above, we got the answers right much more regularly!)
				- We moved the limit of 5 orders from the order history to a maximum of 10 orders for a message.
		</details>
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: We do not support the use of a single email address for multiple stores. One email can be assigned only to one store.**</summary>
			**Why is this important?**
			- **System Identification:** Our system identifies which store an AI Agent should interact with based on the email address from which it receives messages. This ensures that the AI Agent operates with the correct settings for each specific store.
			- <span discussion-urls="{{discussion://ff81704a-66b2-460e-8cee-040aa3d3d79c}}">**Shopify Account Limitations:**</span><span discussion-urls="{{discussion://ff81704a-66b2-460e-8cee-040aa3d3d79c}}"> When a merchant uses a single email address for multiple stores, </span>AI Agent will still respond and try to resolve tickets from Shoppers without a Shopify account, as long as Shopify data is not needed to achieve a full resolution, If Shopify data is needed, then the ticket will be handed over/escalated to the appropriate level of support.
			**Examples:**
			- **Supported Scenario:** If a merchant has two stores, each with its own email address (e.g., store1@merchant.com and store2@merchant.com), both stores can use the AI Agent without any issues.
			- **Unsupported Scenario:** If a merchant uses one email address (e.g., contact@merchant.com) for both stores, we can only set up AI Agent for one of these stores. Our system would not be able to determine the appropriate store settings for incoming messages, and it would not connect shoppers without a Shopify account to the correct store.
		</details>
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: AI agent doesn’t do “reply all” but only “reply”**</summary>
			- Requested [by Gorgias account here]({{slackMessage://gorgias.slack.com/C071ZD1QLGH/1715178852.127299/1715178852.127299}})
			- We need to scope how frequently this is an issue: do customers often cc another email address and if yes should the default behavior be to “reply all”? For now we chose to be cautious by only replying to the email address that was used to send the email to the merchant’s inbox.
		</details>
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: Multi-language capabilities**</summary>
			AI Agent is able to write messages and respond to customers in any language that our LLM supports — over 80+ languages. In order for AI Agent to form the best possible response in another language, there are a few considerations:
				- You will also be able to customize the language AI Agent speaks in in Q2 ‘25 
					- Doc: <mention-page url="{{https://www.notion.so/1061ae2178f580189569fe902f31147f}}"/> 
				- If you choose to [connect a Help Center to AI Agent]({{https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/set-up-and-go-live-with-ai-agent-500219#connect-ai-agent-to-a-help-center}}), we strongly recommend that you have articles written in English (US or GB). This does not mean that you cannot also have articles written in another language, only that you should have an English version published for AI Agent to form the best response in another language.
				- If you [connect AI Agent to a public webpage (URL)]({{https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/set-up-and-go-live-with-ai-agent-500219#connect-ai-agent-to-a-public-webpage}}), the webpage can be written in any language
				Language vs. regions
				- Example: A brand with multiple stores:
					- US store might have knowledge in English and Spanish
					- EU store might have knowledge in English and Spanish
					- But two stores have different support policies.
				- Important for Guidance to be clear on a per store basis so that the AI Agent pulls the correct information depending on both language and region.
					- Language used will take precedent over region. Your proxy for region could just be via a dedicated help center for a store in a different region or using guidance 
		</details>
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: What are the current Actions limitations?**</summary>
			- **Using data from other integrations than Shopify to create conditions (e.g. reading order status from WMS):** we’re working on improving this, but in the meantime a workaround might be possible if the merchant has a time window between the moment the order is created and it’s submitted for fulfillment.
		</details>
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: Assigning a name to AI Agent for purpose of CSAT Survey or in Statistics**</summary>
			- Yes! You can now rename AI Agent’s name 
				- Ref doc: <mention-page url="{{https://www.notion.so/15f1ae2178f5804d8610c5855430f5b3}}"/> 
		</details>
		<details>
		<summary>**Q: Can AI Agent work with Ada chatbot once a ticket is converted into an email?**</summary>
			- AI Agent doesn’t respond to tickets made via the API right now, which is how Ada creates them. AI agent only responds to an actual email sent to their support address. However, the ADA handover ticket is created as an email channel ticket in Gorgias, which can cause confusion.
		</details>
