## ANALYZE AI AGENT

## Explore AI Agent Performance by Ticket Topic

**URL:** https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/explore-ai-agent-performance-by-ticket-topic-1997826

Use the Intents page to understand which types of issues your AI Agent handles best and identify areas where you can improve its performance.

### Requirements

- You must have an active AI Agent subscription
- You must have Lead or Admin permissions

### Access the Intents Page

1. From the main menu, go to AI Agent
2. Click Intents to view all ticket topics AI Agent has been handling
3. The page displays a list of intents (topics) with performance metrics

### Key Metrics Explained

**Intent** — The category or topic of the customer's question (e.g., Returns, Order Tracking, Shipping)

**Automation rate** — The percentage of conversations where AI Agent fully resolved the issue without human intervention

**Ticket volume** — The number of tickets AI Agent handled for this intent

**Average CSAT** — Customer satisfaction score for AI Agent's responses on this topic

**Handover rate** — The percentage of conversations where AI Agent handed over to a human agent

### Use Intents to Improve AI Agent

1. **Identify strong performance areas** — Look for intents with high automation rates and CSAT scores. Understand why AI Agent performs well on these topics
2. **Find improvement opportunities** — Focus on intents with lower automation rates or handover rates. These are areas where AI Agent needs more knowledge
3. **Create targeted Guidance** — For low-performing intents, create new Guidance to teach AI Agent how to handle those scenarios
4. **Add complementary content** — Update Help Center articles or add new knowledge sources related to underperforming intents
5. **Monitor progress** — After making changes, return to the Intents page to track whether automation rate has improved

### Filter and Sort Results

- **Date range** — View performance for specific time periods
- **Store** — If you have multiple stores, view intents for specific stores
- **Sort options** — Sort by automation rate, ticket volume, or CSAT to quickly identify priorities

## Review Automation Performance in AI Agent Overview Report

**URL:** https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/review-automation-performance-in-the-ai-agent-overview-report-1997827

The AI Agent Overview Report provides a high-level summary of your AI Agent's performance across all channels and topics.

### Requirements

- You must have an active AI Agent subscription
- You must have Lead or Admin permissions

### Access the Overview Report

1. From the main menu, go to Statistics
2. Navigate to AI Agent > Overview
3. The Overview Report displays key performance metrics

### Key Metrics in the Overview Report

**Automation rate** — The percentage of tickets completely resolved by AI Agent without human intervention

**Automated interactions** — The total number of conversations AI Agent fully resolved

**Cost savings** — Estimated cost savings from automated interactions (calculated based on average agent cost)

**Total conversations** — The total number of conversations AI Agent participated in

**Handover rate** — The percentage of conversations AI Agent handed over to human agents

**Average first-response time** — How quickly AI Agent responds to customer inquiries

**Average CSAT** — Overall customer satisfaction with AI Agent's responses

### Use the Overview Report to Guide Strategy

1. **Track progress over time** — Compare metrics week-over-week or month-over-month to measure improvements
2. **Set targets** — Use these metrics to establish automation rate goals and track progress toward them
3. **Identify trends** — Look for patterns in the data (e.g., automation rate declining or improving)
4. **Communicate impact** — Use these metrics to demonstrate the value of AI Agent to stakeholders
5. **Prioritize improvements** — Use CSAT and handover rate to identify where to focus your training efforts

## How AI Agent Recommends Products and Offers Discounts

**URL:** https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/how-ai-agent-recommends-products-and-offers-discounts-1997831

AI Agent's Shopping Assistant uses intelligent algorithms and your product and discount strategies to make personalized product recommendations and offer discounts to shoppers.

### How AI Agent Recommends Products

**Analyze customer context** — AI Agent considers the customer's:
- Browsing history (what they've been looking at)
- Purchase history (what they've bought before)
- Current conversation (what they're asking about)

**Consider product attributes** — AI Agent evaluates:
- Product popularity and trending items
- Complementary products that pair well with what the customer is interested in
- Upsell opportunities (premium alternatives or add-ons)
- Product availability and inventory status

**Generate relevant recommendations** — Based on this analysis, AI Agent recommends products that are most likely to be relevant and valuable to the individual shopper.

**Personalize the pitch** — AI Agent explains WHY it's recommending each product, using natural language that connects the recommendation to the customer's needs.

### How AI Agent Offers Discounts

**Applies your discount strategy** — AI Agent uses your configured discount strategy to decide:
- When to offer a discount
- Which products are eligible for discounts
- How large the discount should be

**Strategic timing** — AI Agent offers discounts at moments when they're most likely to influence a purchase decision, such as:
- When a customer is hesitating about a purchase
- When they're comparing products
- When they're about to leave the conversation without buying

**Personalized offers** — AI Agent considers customer segments and loyalty status to provide personalized discount offers:
- VIP customers might receive higher discounts
- New customers might receive welcome discounts
- Returning customers might receive loyalty-based offers

## Customize Your Shopping Assistant Selling and Discount Strategy

**URL:** https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/customize-your-shopping-assistant-selling-and-discount-strategy-1997832

You can customize how aggressively AI Agent sells and how it uses discounts to encourage purchases.

### Requirements

- You must have an active AI Agent subscription
- Shopping Assistant must be enabled
- You must have Lead or Admin permissions

### Access Shopping Assistant Strategy Settings

1. From the main menu, go to AI Agent
2. Click Shopping Assistant > Strategy
3. Adjust your selling and discount settings

### Selling Style Options

AI Agent can adopt different selling approaches:

**Educational** — AI Agent focuses on explaining product features and benefits without aggressive selling. Best for customers who want to make informed decisions.

**Balanced** — AI Agent balances being helpful with encouraging purchases. Recommends relevant products and offers discounts naturally.

**Promotional** — AI Agent actively encourages purchases and makes frequent product recommendations. Best for driving sales volume.

### Discount Strategy Options

Configure how AI Agent uses discounts:

**Conservative** — Only offer discounts in specific situations or to certain customer segments

**Moderate** — Offer discounts regularly but strategically to influence purchases

**Aggressive** — Use discounts frequently to maximize conversion rates

### Set Discount Parameters

- **Discount eligibility** — Which products can receive discounts
- **Discount amount range** — Minimum and maximum discount percentages
- **Maximum discount per customer** — How many discounts a single customer can receive
- **Customer segment rules** — Different discount strategies for different customer types

### Test and Optimize

1. Deploy your initial strategy
2. Monitor Shopping Assistant analytics for conversion rate and average order value
3. Adjust strategy based on performance
4. Continue testing to find the optimal balance for your brand

## Control Which Products AI Agent Recommends to Shoppers

**URL:** https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/control-which-products-ai-agent-recommends-to-shoppers-1997833

You can control exactly which products AI Agent is allowed to recommend and in what priority.

### Requirements

- You must have an active AI Agent subscription
- Shopping Assistant must be enabled
- You must have Lead or Admin permissions

### Access Product Recommendation Settings

1. From the main menu, go to AI Agent
2. Click Shopping Assistant > Product Recommendations
3. View and configure your product recommendation strategy

### Configure Recommendation Rules

**Include/Exclude by category** — Allow AI Agent to recommend products from specific categories

**Whitelist premium products** — Prioritize high-margin or promotional products for recommendations

**Exclude low-stock items** — Prevent AI Agent from recommending items with low inventory

**Exclude seasonal items** — Temporarily exclude seasonal products from recommendations

**Prioritize new products** — Have AI Agent recommend new or recently launched products

### Set Recommendation Priorities

- **High priority** — Products you want AI Agent to recommend most frequently
- **Standard priority** — Regular product recommendations
- **Low priority** — Products recommended only in specific contexts

### Filter by Product Attributes

Filter which products are eligible for recommendations based on:
- Price range
- Product type/category
- Availability status
- Inventory level
- Custom tags or attributes

### Refresh Recommendations

Product recommendations update automatically as you make changes to your product catalog or recommendation rules.

## Turn On Customer Engagement Features for AI Agent

**URL:** https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/turn-on-customer-engagement-features-for-ai-agent-1997834

Customer Engagement features encourage shoppers to start conversations with AI Agent proactively, rather than waiting for customers to reach out first.

### Requirements

- You must have an active AI Agent subscription
- Shopping Assistant must be enabled
- You must have Lead or Admin permissions

### Access Customer Engagement Settings

1. From the main menu, go to AI Agent
2. Click Shopping Assistant > Customer Engagement
3. Enable and configure engagement features

### Available Engagement Features

**Suggested Product Questions** — AI Agent suggests product-related questions to shoppers based on what they're browsing
- Encourages conversation about specific products
- Helps overcome shopping hesitations
- Example: "Is this machine washable?"

**Ask Anything Input** — Encourages customers to ask AI Agent any question
- Creates low-friction entry point to chat
- Invites customers to engage directly

**Trigger on Search** — AI Agent initiates a conversation when customers use search on your site
- Offers help with product discovery
- Proactive assistance based on search intent

**Browse Abandonment** — AI Agent reaches out when customers are browsing without purchasing
- Offers help or product recommendations
- Attempts to re-engage before customer leaves

**Back-in-Stock Notifications** — AI Agent notifies customers when products they viewed are back in stock
- Re-engages previously interested customers
- Drives repeat visits

### Configure Engagement Rules

For each engagement feature, you can set:

- **When to trigger** — Under what conditions should this feature activate
- **Message** — What message AI Agent sends to initiate the conversation
- **Target audience** — Which customer types (new, returning, VIP) should see this feature
- **Frequency** — How often to show this engagement feature to avoid overload

### Monitor Engagement Performance

1. Go to Statistics > Shopping Assistant
2. Review engagement metrics to see:
   - How many conversations were initiated by each feature
   - Conversion rates from engaged customers
   - Which engagement features drive the most sales

3. Optimize based on performance

## Upgrade Your AI Agent Subscription to Use Shopping Assistant Skills

**URL:** https://docs.gorgias.com/en-US/upgrade-your-ai-agent-subscription-to-use-shopping-assistant-skills-1997835

Shopping Assistant capabilities are available with premium AI Agent subscriptions. If you subscribed to AI Agent before July 16, 2025, you may need to upgrade to access Shopping Assistant features.

### Requirements

- Active AI Agent subscription
- Shopify store connected to Gorgias
- Owner or Account Owner permissions to manage subscriptions

### What's Included in Shopping Assistant

With a Shopping Assistant subscription upgrade, you get:

- **Product Recommendations** — AI Agent recommends products to shoppers
- **Discount Strategy** — AI Agent offers personalized discounts to encourage purchases
- **Sales Conversation Support** — AI Agent helps shoppers find products and close sales
- **Customer Engagement Features** — Proactive features to encourage conversations
- **Shopping Analytics** — Metrics showing influenced revenue and conversion impact

### Check Your Subscription Status

1. From the main menu, go to Account > Billing
2. Review your current AI Agent subscription plan
3. Check if Shopping Assistant is included

### Upgrade to Shopping Assistant

1. From the main menu, go to Account > Billing
2. Click Manage Subscription or Upgrade
3. Select the Shopping Assistant plan option
4. Follow the upgrade process to activate

### What Happens After Upgrade

Once upgraded, Shopping Assistant features will be immediately available:

- Access Shopping Assistant configuration pages (Strategy, Product Recommendations, Customer Engagement)
- AI Agent will begin making product recommendations and offering discounts in customer conversations
- Shopping Assistant analytics will become available in your Statistics reports
- You can begin optimizing your sales strategy immediately

For current pricing information and plan details, contact Gorgias Sales or refer to your account dashboard.
