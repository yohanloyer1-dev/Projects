# 🎯 Client Discovery Questions
## Kickoff Call Checklist

**Purpose:** Gather all required information before audit begins
**Timing:** Pre-audit kickoff call with client
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes
**Output:** Complete client brief ready for data collection phase

---

## Section 1: Context & Goals (5 min)

### Background
- [ ] **What's your primary business model?** (e-commerce, SaaS, marketplace, etc.)
- [ ] **How long have you been using Gorgias?** (helps understand maturity level)
- [ ] **What problem are we solving today?** (capture their perspective)
- [ ] **What does success look like to you?** (extract their definition of success)

### Current State
- [ ] **How much support do you currently handle?** (in-house vs. outsourced)
- [ ] **What's your biggest customer support challenge right now?**
- [ ] **Do you have any concerns or hesitations about AI automation?** (address them now)

---

## Section 2: Financial & Volume Data (8 min)

### Current Outsourcing Costs
- [ ] **Do you use an outsourced support partner?** If yes, which one?
- [ ] **What's your monthly subscription cost with them?** (if any)
- [ ] **What's the cost per ticket?** (variable cost)
- [ ] **What's your average monthly support ticket volume?** (ask for last 6 months to get average)
- [ ] **Are there seasonal fluctuations?** (holiday peaks, summer lows, etc.)
- [ ] **Can you share the last 3-6 months of invoices?** (gives exact figures)

### In-House Costs (if applicable)
- [ ] **How many support team members do you have?**
- [ ] **What's their average monthly salary/cost?**
- [ ] **How much time do they spend on support vs. other tasks?**

### Key Metric: Total Monthly Support Cost
```
= (Outsourcing subscription) + (Tickets × Cost per ticket)
  OR (Team size × Monthly salary × % time on support)
```
**Example:** €299 + (5,700 tickets × €2.05) = €12,000/month

---

## Section 3: Tech Stack & Integrations (7 min)

### Gorgias Setup
- [ ] **Which email integration are you using?** (Gmail, Outlook, IMAP, etc.)
- [ ] **Do you have Chat deployed?** (on website, mobile, etc.)
- [ ] **Do you have any other channels?** (SMS, Instagram DM, WhatsApp, etc.)
- [ ] **What's your primary store platform?** (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.)

### Business Tools
- [ ] **What's your shipping/fulfillment system?** (Shopify native, ShipStation, ShipBob, etc.)
- [ ] **How do you handle returns?** (manual, Loop, Returnly, etc.)
- [ ] **Do you have a subscription system?** (Skio, Cratejoy, Subbly, etc.)
- [ ] **What payment processor do you use?** (Stripe, Square, etc.)
- [ ] **Do you track customer data anywhere?** (CRM, analytics, etc.)

### Knowledge Base
- [ ] **Do you have a Help Center set up?** (Gorgias or external)
- [ ] **How many articles/pages do you have?**
- [ ] **What topics are covered?** (Returns, shipping, product care, etc.)
- [ ] **Do you have any other knowledge sources?** (Notion, Google Docs, etc.)

---

## Section 4: Policies & Business Rules (8 min)

### Returns & Refunds
- [ ] **What's your return window?** (e.g., 30 days)
- [ ] **Who pays for return shipping?**
- [ ] **What's your refund process?** (immediate, after item received, etc.)
- [ ] **Are there any exceptions?** (final sale items, clearance, etc.)
- [ ] **Can you automate refunds** (refund APIs, manual approval needed, etc.)

### Shipping & Delivery
- [ ] **What carriers do you use?** (FedEx, UPS, DHL, etc.)
- [ ] **What are typical delivery times?** (by region)
- [ ] **What do you do for lost/damaged packages?**
- [ ] **Do you offer tracked shipping?** (automatically provided)

### Product-Specific Rules
- [ ] **Are there products with special handling?** (fragile, hazardous, etc.)
- [ ] **Do you have warranty/guarantee policies?**
- [ ] **Are there products that can't be returned?** (hygiene items, custom orders)
- [ ] **Do you offer exchanges?** (instead of refunds)

### Customer Support Rules
- [ ] **What topics require human escalation?** (angry customers, exceptions, etc.)
- [ ] **Do you offer discounts/codes for complaints?** (if so, who authorizes)
- [ ] **What's your tone/voice policy?** (formal, friendly, brand-specific, etc.)
- [ ] **Are there any brand partnerships we should know about?** (resellers, wholesalers)

---

## Section 5: Automation Goals (5 min)

### Current State
- [ ] **What percentage of support do you think could be automated?** (their estimate)
- [ ] **Which types of requests do you currently automate?** (or try to)
- [ ] **What's been tried before?** (and why it didn't work, if relevant)

### Target State
- [ ] **What's your ideal automation rate?** (should steer toward 30-50% for realistic targets)
  - < 30% = Too conservative, missing opportunities
  - 30-50% = Sweet spot for most businesses
  - > 50% = Excellent, requires significant optimization
- [ ] **Are there specific intents you want to prioritize?** (order status, returns, etc.)
- [ ] **What would be a "win" for you in the first month?** (helps set expectations)

### Risk/Concern Mitigation
- [ ] **What are you worried might go wrong?** (listen for concerns)
- [ ] **How important is brand voice consistency?** (affects guidance complexity)
- [ ] **Do you need human approval for refunds?** (determines automation ceiling)

---

## Section 6: Timeline & Logistics (3 min)

### Project Timeline
- [ ] **When do you want this completed?** (realistic: 4 weeks for full audit + implementation)
- [ ] **Do you have key dates?** (product launches, sales, etc.)
- [ ] **Who's the point of contact for the project?** (name, email, availability)

### Data & Access
- [ ] **Can you export 60 days of ticket data?** (needed for analysis)
- [ ] **Do you have screenshot capabilities?** (for Gorgias dashboard)
- [ ] **Can you share your Help Center?** (need to review existing content)
- [ ] **Who approves decisions?** (you, team, stakeholder, etc.)

### Meeting Schedule
- [ ] **When should we do our next call?** (to present findings)
- [ ] **Who else should be on that call?** (technical team, marketing, CEO, etc.)
- [ ] **What format do you prefer?** (presentation, demo, written report, etc.)

---

## Section 7: Brand & Communication (3 min)

### Brand Identity
- [ ] **What's your brand personality?** (luxury, playful, professional, etc.)
- [ ] **Any specific tone requirements?** (French/English, formal/informal, etc.)
- [ ] **Do you have brand guidelines we should follow?**
- [ ] **Any words/phrases you want to avoid or include?**

### Stakeholders
- [ ] **Who will use the AI Agent system?** (support team, manager, etc.)
- [ ] **Who makes decisions?** (different from daily user?)
- [ ] **Who else should we keep informed?** (CFO, operations, etc.)

## After Call: Send This

Create a **client brief** summarizing:
1. ✅ Current support volume and costs
2. ✅ Monthly savings opportunity (rough estimate)
3. ✅ Key metrics to track (automation rate, coverage, cost)
4. ✅ Timeline and next steps
5. ✅ Data you need by [date]

**Example email closing:**
> "Great call! Here's what I heard: You're at ~€12K/month with 5,700 tickets. Moving from 8% to 30% automation would save roughly €1,500-2,000/month. I'll need the attached data by [date] to start the audit. See you on [date] to review findings!"

---

## Red Flags to Watch For

🚩 **Can't get financial data** → Might indicate disorganization or budget constraints
🚩 **No Help Center or documentation** → Means more guidance creation work upfront
🚩 **Refunds require CEO approval** → Limits automation ceiling significantly
🚩 **No expectation setting** → May lead to unrealistic goals (80%+ automation)
🚩 **Multiple stakeholders, no clear decision maker** → Decision paralysis risk

---

## Green Lights to Celebrate

✅ **Complete financial records** → Professional, organized client
✅ **Already using some Gorgias features** → Faster implementation
✅ **Clear brand guidelines** → Easier guidance creation
✅ **Engaged stakeholders on call** → Buy-in from the start
✅ **Realistic goals** (30-50% automation) → Sets up for success
