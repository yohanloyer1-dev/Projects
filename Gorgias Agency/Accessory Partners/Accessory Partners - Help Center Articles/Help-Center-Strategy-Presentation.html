1.1 How do I track my order?

Get your tracking number and check your delivery status.

Quick Answer

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your package. Most orders placed before 2 PM Arizona time ship the same day.

How It Works

  1. Check your email — Look for a shipping confirmation from Suspension Superstore with your tracking number
  2. Click the tracking link — This takes you directly to the carrier's tracking page (UPS, FedEx, etc.)
  3. No email yet? — Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. If it's been longer, contact us with your order number

Common Issues

  • Tracking not updating — Carriers can take 24-48 hours to scan packages after pickup. If tracking hasn't updated in 3+ days, contact us.
  • Delivered but not received — Check around your property, with neighbors, or building management. If still missing, contact us immediately.

Still Need Help?

Contact us with: Order number, Email address used for the order

1.2 How long does shipping take?

Shipping times, free shipping policy, and delivery estimates.

Quick Answer

Most orders ship same day if placed before 2 PM Arizona time (Monday–Friday). Transit time is typically 2-5 business days depending on your location.

How It Works

Free shipping is available on most orders to the contiguous United States (lower 48 states).

Estimated transit times from our warehouses:

  • Arizona, Texas, California — 2-4 business days to most US locations
  • Pennsylvania — 2-3 business days to East Coast

Factors that affect shipping time:

  • Orders placed after 2 PM AZ time or on weekends ship the next business day
  • Drop-ship items ship directly from the manufacturer (may take 3-7 business days)
  • Backorder or special order items will have a separate ETA provided

Common Issues

  • Hawaii, Alaska, Puerto Rico — Free shipping does not apply. Additional shipping charges will be quoted.
  • Weekend orders — Orders placed Saturday/Sunday ship Monday.

1.3 Do you ship internationally?

International shipping availability and costs.

Quick Answer

Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your destination and order weight.

How It Works

  1. Add items to cart and proceed to checkout
  2. Enter your shipping address — International shipping options and costs will display
  3. Complete your order — You are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees charged by your country

Common Issues

  • Customs/duties — These fees are collected by your country, not by us. We cannot predict or prepay these charges.
  • Delivery times — International orders typically take 7-21 business days depending on destination and customs processing.
  • Restricted items — Some products cannot be shipped internationally due to manufacturer restrictions.

1.4 My order hasn't shipped yet

What to do if your order is delayed or on backorder.

Quick Answer

Most in-stock orders ship within 1-2 business days. If your order is delayed, it may be a drop-ship item, on backorder, or awaiting verification. Check your email for updates or contact us with your order number.

Common Reasons for Delays

  1. Drop-ship items — These ship directly from the manufacturer. Typical lead time is 3-7 business days, sometimes longer for specialty items.
  2. Backorder — The item is temporarily out of stock. We'll email you with an estimated ship date.
  3. Order verification — Your order may be on hold pending payment verification (see "Why is my order on hold?")
  4. Special order — Some items are built or configured to order and have longer lead times.

1.5 Why is my order on hold?

Order verification for flagged transactions.

Quick Answer

Some orders are flagged for verification when the billing and shipping information doesn't match, or the transaction is flagged as potentially fraudulent. This is for your protection.

How It Works

  1. You'll receive an email asking you to complete a verification form
  2. Fill out the form at suspensionsuperstore.com/order-verification/
  3. Once verified, your order will be released and shipped

Common triggers: Billing address mismatch, different shipping address, new credit card, using someone else's card

2.1 How do I return a part?

Start a return and get your RMA number.

Quick Answer

Submit a return request within 30 days of receiving your order. You'll receive an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number to include on your package.

How It Works

  1. Submit a return request at suspensionsuperstore.com/return-authorization-request/
  2. Receive your RMA number via email (usually within 24 hours)
  3. Write the RMA number on the outside of the box — Returns without an RMA may be refused
  4. Ship the item back to the address provided in your RMA email
  5. Refund or exchange processed within 24-48 hours of receiving your return

Common Issues

  • Item must be NEW and uninstalled — Parts that have been installed or show signs of use cannot be returned for refund.
  • Original packaging required — Keep all packaging until you're sure you won't need to return.

2.2 What is your return policy?

Return window, conditions, and fees explained.

Quick Answer

Returns must be initiated within 30 days of receipt. Items must be in NEW, uninstalled condition. A 20% restocking fee applies to refunds, plus you pay return shipping.

How It Works

For refunds:

  • 20% restocking fee applies
  • You pay shipping both ways (original and return)
  • Refund = price paid minus shipping and restocking fee

For exchanges or store credit:

  • No restocking fee
  • You pay return shipping only
  • Full credit for the item price

No fees if we made a mistake: Wrong item shipped, item arrived damaged, defective product

2.3 Can I exchange for a different part?

Exchange process and what to expect.

Quick Answer

Yes, you can exchange for a different part. Submit a return request and select "Exchange" as your preference. No restocking fee applies to exchanges.

How It Works

  1. Submit a return request at suspensionsuperstore.com/return-authorization-request/
  2. Tell us what you need instead — Include the correct part number or describe what you're looking for
  3. Ship the original item back — You pay return shipping
  4. We ship your replacement — Once your return is in transit back to us, we'll ship the new item

Price difference: If the new part costs more, we'll send a payment link. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference.

2.4 How do I cancel my order?

Cancel before shipment and refund timing.

Quick Answer

Contact us as soon as possible to cancel. If your order hasn't shipped yet, we can cancel and issue a full refund. Once shipped, you'll need to return it.

How It Works

  1. Contact us immediately — Call 866-409-8673 or email for fastest response
  2. If not yet shipped — We'll cancel and refund in full
  3. If already shipped — You'll need to either refuse delivery or submit a return request once received

Refund timing: Allow 24-48 hours for your bank to process the refund after cancellation.

2.5 How long do refunds take?

Refund processing time after return received.

Quick Answer

Refunds are processed within 24-48 hours of receiving your return. Your bank may take an additional 3-5 business days to post the credit to your account.

Refund Amount

  • Item price minus 20% restocking fee (if applicable)
  • Original shipping is not refunded

3.1 Will this part fit my vehicle?

What we need to verify fitment (Year/Make/Model/Trim).

Quick Answer

Fitment depends on your specific vehicle. To verify compatibility, we need your Year, Make, Model, and Trim level. For lift or lowering kits, we also need your current lift height and intended use (daily driving, off-road, towing).

Information We Need

  • Year (e.g., 2024)
  • Make (e.g., Ford)
  • Model (e.g., F-150)
  • Trim/Package (e.g., XLT, Lariat, Raptor)
  • 2WD or 4WD
  • Current suspension setup (stock, lifted, lowered)
  • Tire size you plan to run

Where to find your trim level: Driver's door jamb sticker, vehicle registration, or owner's manual.

Common Issues

  • Trim matters — A base model and premium trim may have different suspension components.
  • 2WD vs 4WD — Many parts are not interchangeable between 2WD and 4WD versions.
  • Modified vehicles — If your truck is already lifted or lowered, this affects what parts will work.

3.2 Where are installation instructions?

Find install guides and videos for your kit.

Quick Answer

Installation instructions are available on our website and from each manufacturer. Visit our Installation Instructions page or check the manufacturer's website for your specific kit.

How to Find Instructions

  1. Visit suspensionsuperstore.com/installation-instructions/
  2. Search by brand or part number
  3. Download the PDF or watch video guides

Manufacturer resources: Most kits include printed instructions in the box. Manufacturers often have video tutorials on YouTube. Search "[Brand name] [Part number] install" for community guides.

⚠️ Category 5: Shipping Problems

3 articles targeting ~126 tickets (Quick resolution for shipping issues)

5.1 My order arrived damaged

Report damage and get a replacement.

Quick Answer

Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll work with you to get a replacement shipped as quickly as possible.

How It Works

  1. Take photos — Document the damaged packaging AND the damaged parts before discarding anything
  2. Contact us — Email or text us with your order number and photos
  3. We file a claim — We handle the shipping claim on your behalf
  4. Replacement shipped — Once approved, we ship your replacement (often same day)

Important: Keep all original packaging until the claim is resolved. The carrier may need to inspect it.

What to Photograph

  • Outside of the shipping box (all sides showing damage)
  • Inside packaging
  • The damaged part(s)
  • Shipping label

5.2 My order is missing parts

Report missing hardware or components.

Quick Answer

Contact us with your order number and what's missing. We'll ship the missing parts right away.

How It Works

  1. Double-check the packaging — Sometimes small hardware bags are tucked inside larger boxes or wrapped in padding
  2. Contact us — Let us know your order number and exactly what's missing
  3. We ship replacements — Missing parts are typically shipped same or next business day

Common Missing Items

  • Hardware bags (bolts, nuts, U-bolts)
  • Instruction sheets
  • Smaller components bundled separately

5.3 I received the wrong item

What to do if you got the wrong part.

Quick Answer

Contact us with your order number and photos of what you received. We'll ship the correct item and arrange return of the wrong one at no cost to you.

How It Works

  1. Contact us — Provide your order number and photos of the item you received
  2. We verify the error — We'll check what was ordered vs. what shipped
  3. Correct item shipped — We ship the right part, often same day
  4. Return the wrong item — We'll send a prepaid return label for the incorrect part

No fees: If we shipped the wrong item, there's no restocking fee and we cover return shipping.
