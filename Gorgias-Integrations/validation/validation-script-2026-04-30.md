# Validation Scripts — ManoMano → Gorgias Integration
**Date:** 2026-04-30 | **Session:** master-prompt-V1 | **Environment:** Cowork

---

## Context

First integration: ManoMano → Gorgias channel integration (polling/push service).
Goal: 5 conversations (3 merchants, 2 Gorgias agencies) before any build work begins.
Pass threshold: ≥3/5 confirm pain + WTP ≥€50/mo (merchants) or setup fee ≥€500 (agencies).

Do NOT reveal the product idea. Confirm demand, don't create it.

---

## Script A: Merchant Version

**Who to target:** French brands on Gorgias Basic or Pro who sell on ManoMano.
**Format:** 20-min call or async written exchange. Frame as "quick feedback on your support workflow."

---

**Opening (set context, no pitch):**
"Hey [Name], I'm doing some research on how Gorgias merchants who sell on French marketplaces manage their support workflow. Would you be up for 4–5 quick questions? Takes about 10 minutes, no pitch, just trying to understand the friction."

---

**Q1 — Current state:**
"How do you currently handle customer messages that come in from ManoMano? Is it managed inside Gorgias, or in a separate tool?"

*What you're listening for:* "We manage it separately" / "we have to log into the seller portal" / "it's a mess" = green light. "We have it in Gorgias" = ask how.

---

**Q2 — Agent context:**
"When a support agent opens a ticket from a ManoMano customer, what information do they wish they had right there — that they currently have to look up manually somewhere else?"

*Listening for:* Order status, customer message history, return status, SLA deadline.

---

**Q3 — Time cost:**
"Rough estimate — how much time per week does your team spend switching between Gorgias and ManoMano's seller portal to handle support?"

*Listening for:* Anything >30 min/week = clear ROI case.

---

**Q4 — WTP anchor (key question):**
"If everything from ManoMano — customer messages, order info — showed up in Gorgias automatically as tickets, what would that kind of tool be worth to you per month? Just a rough ballpark."

*Listening for:* €50+/mo = validation. Less than €30 = price sensitivity (pivot to lower-cost display widget). "A lot" without a number = probe further.

---

**Q5 — Stakeholder map:**
"If something like that existed, who else on your team would have an opinion on it — support lead, ops, finance?"

*Listening for:* Identifies the real buyer and internal champion.

---

**Closing:**
"That's really helpful, thank you. I'm exploring whether to build something in this space. If I put together a simple demo, would you be open to seeing it?"

---

## Script B: Agency Version

**Who to target:** Gorgias-specialist agencies or ecommerce growth agencies with French merchant clients on ManoMano.
**Format:** 20-min call. Frame as "picking your brain on a gap you probably see with clients."

---

**Opening:**
"Hey [Name], I know you work with a lot of Gorgias merchants — I'm doing some research on a specific friction point and think you'd have a sharp take on it. 4–5 questions, 10 minutes. No pitch."

---

**Q1 — Client landscape:**
"Of your Gorgias clients who sell on French marketplaces like ManoMano or Cdiscount — how do they currently handle support for those channels? Is it in Gorgias or managed separately?"

*Listening for:* "Most of them manage it separately" = strong signal. "Some have it in Gorgias via [tool]" = probe which tool.

---

**Q2 — Client requests:**
"Has any client ever asked you to help connect ManoMano to Gorgias, or complained about that gap? What did you tell them?"

*Listening for:* "Yes, we had to tell them there's no solution" = very strong. "Nobody's asked" = softer signal, probe why.

---

**Q3 — Implementation value:**
"If a reliable, done-for-you ManoMano → Gorgias integration existed — something you could add to your implementation packages — would that be valuable to your clients? What would you charge for setting it up?"

*Listening for:* "Yes, we'd include it in onboarding" + any €500+ setup fee = green. "Maybe" = probe what would make it certain.

---

**Q4 — Build vs. buy:**
"Have you ever considered building something like this yourselves? What stopped you?"

*Listening for:* "Too much dev work / maintenance burden" = confirms agency moat. "We started but..." = understand what they learned.

---

**Q5 — Arrangement preference:**
"If you were recommending a tool like this to your clients, would you prefer a one-time finder's fee, a monthly revenue share per merchant, or both?"

*Listening for:* Revenue share preferred = recurring relationship, stickier. One-time preferred = simpler to manage for them.

---

**Closing:**
"Super useful. I'm seriously considering building this — if I put together an early access program for agencies, would you want to be one of the first to offer it to clients?"
