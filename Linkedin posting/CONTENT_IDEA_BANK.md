## SERIES IDEAS (Recurring Formats)

### "Gorgias Tip of the Week"
Short text + optional screen recording. One specific Gorgias feature, rule, or automation per post. Becomes a go-to series for Gorgias users.

### "Stat I Wish More Brands Knew"
One CX or e-commerce stat + your take on what it means. Your "statistics are underused" belief turned into recurring content.

### "What I Saw This Week"
Anonymised observation from your work managing agency partners. "Had a conversation this week with an agency..." Pattern is real, specific, insider.

### "Before / After"
Anonymised brand transformation. Simple format: problem → audit → result. No names, no fake metrics, just the story shape.

### "Gorgias Product Update Take"
Every time Gorgias ships something new, you comment on it with your insider angle. Positions you as THE reference for Gorgias interpretation.

### "CX Teardown"
Take a brand's public CX setup (help centre, chat, policies) and review it publicly. Educational, entertaining, high-engagement.
