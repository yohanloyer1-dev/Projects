# Category 1: Orders & Shipping

## 1.1 How do I track my order?

Get your tracking number and check your delivery status.

### Quick answer

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your package. Most orders placed before 2 PM Arizona time ship the same day.

### How it works

1. **Check your email** — Look for a shipping confirmation from Suspension Superstore with your tracking number
2. **Click the tracking link** — This takes you directly to the carrier's tracking page (UPS, FedEx, etc.)
3. **No email yet?** — Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. If it's been longer, contact us with your order number

### Common issues

- **Tracking not updating** — Carriers can take 24-48 hours to scan packages after pickup. If tracking hasn't updated in 3+ days, contact us.
- **Delivered but not received** — Check around your property, with neighbors, or building management. If still missing, contact us immediately.

### Still need help?

Contact us with:
- Order number
- Email address used for the order

- **Weekend orders** — Orders placed Saturday/Sunday ship Monday.

## 2.2 What is your return policy?

Return window, conditions, and fees explained.

### Quick answer

Returns must be initiated within **30 days** of receipt. Items must be in NEW, uninstalled condition. A **20% restocking fee** applies to refunds, plus you pay return shipping.

### How it works

**For refunds:**
- 20% restocking fee applies
- You pay shipping both ways (original and return)
- Refund = price paid minus shipping and restocking fee

**For exchanges or store credit:**
- No restocking fee
- You pay return shipping only
- Full credit for the item price

**No fees if we made a mistake:**
- Wrong item shipped
- Item arrived damaged
- Defective product

### Common issues

- **Installed parts** — Cannot be returned. Even partially installed parts are not eligible.
- **Special orders** — May have different return policies communicated at time of purchase.

### Still need help?

Contact us with:
- Order number
- What you'd like to do (refund, exchange, or store credit)

## 3.1 Will this part fit my vehicle?

What we need to verify fitment (Year/Make/Model/Trim).

### Quick answer

Fitment depends on your specific vehicle. To verify compatibility, we need your **Year, Make, Model, and Trim level**. For lift or lowering kits, we also need your **current lift height** and **intended use** (daily driving, off-road, towing).

### How it works

**Information we need to verify fitment:**
- Year (e.g., 2024)
- Make (e.g., Ford)
- Model (e.g., F-150)
- Trim/Package (e.g., XLT, Lariat, Raptor)
- 2WD or 4WD
- Current suspension setup (stock, lifted, lowered)
- Tire size you plan to run

**Where to find your trim level:**
- Driver's door jamb sticker
- Vehicle registration
- Owner's manual

### Common issues

- **Trim matters** — A base model and a premium trim may have different suspension components.
- **2WD vs 4WD** — Many parts are not interchangeable between 2WD and 4WD versions.
- **Modified vehicles** — If your truck is already lifted or lowered, this affects what parts will work.

### Still need help?

Contact us with:
- Year / Make / Model / Trim
- 2WD or 4WD
- Current setup (stock, lifted X inches, lowered X inches)
- What you're trying to achieve
