# Market Research — Claude OS

*Researched: 2026-04-20*

## Direct Competitors

### getclaudeos.com — Closest competitor
- 33 specialist Claude modes + 158 skill files + MASTER_MEMORY.md
- Simulates persistent context within Claude
- Price: **$49 one-time** — pure DIY, no support, no customization
- Positioning: "Turn Claude from a chatbot into a business operator"
- Free tools as lead magnets
- **Gap: No setup help, no personalization, no consulting**

### OpenClaw for Entrepreneurs (openclawforentrepreneurs.com)
- Claims: 1,000 agents, 36 channels, autonomous execution
- Price: $20–$297/month or $2,500–$7,500 one-time (white-glove)
- Heavy on claims, unknown traction, vague delivery
- **Gap: Software-dependent, unclear ROI, no proof**

## Adjacent Markets

### Notion OS / Second Brain
- August Bradley PPV Life OS: $249 one-time — started Notion Life OS category in 2020
- Forte Labs Second Brain Notion template: ~$100, 27,000+ downloads
- Dozens of Gumroad templates: $9–$79
- **Common failure: 40min/day maintaining the system, abandoned within weeks**
- **Gap: No done-for-you implementation service at human scale**

### Enterprise AI Consulting
- $100–$500/hour at high end
- $30K–$150K for enterprise implementations
- Not packaged for solo operators
- **Gap: Nothing in $500–$2,500 range with real proof**

## The Market Gap (3 things nobody does)

1. **Vertical specificity** — everything is generic "any entrepreneur." First mover in a niche (e-commerce, CX agency, freelance consultant) owns it.

2. **Done-with-you at human scale** — Claude OS is $49 DIY. OpenClaw's $7,500 is vague. Nothing exists at $500–$2,500: "I set it up for your specific business, in your tools, with your context."

3. **Proof and transparency** — every product leans on theory or aspirational copy. No screenshots, no session logs, no real automation rates, no real numbers. Building in public with real client results = clear positioning advantage.

## Opportunity Statement

A verticalized, done-with-you Claude OS for a specific operator profile (freelance consultants, CX agency owners, e-commerce operators), priced $500–$2,500 with visible proof of results — genuinely differentiated from everything in market.
