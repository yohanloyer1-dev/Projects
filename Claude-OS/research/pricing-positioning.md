## Delivery Models

### Done-For-You (DFY) — Highest ticket
- You build everything. Client gets a working system.
- Best margin per client, hardest to scale past 5–8 clients solo.
- Price: $2,500–$5,000 setup + retainer.

### Done-With-You (DWY) — Sweet spot
- 3 sessions, client does work with your guidance.
- Scales better, creates more client ownership.
- Price: $500–$1,500. Most common $50K–$100K/year model.

### Self-Serve — Top-of-funnel / audience monetization
- Template + instructions. Volume play.
- Works as lead magnet or entry product.
- Price: $97–$197.

## Positioning That Converts

Top performers don't sell "AI setup." They sell outcomes:
- "Handle 30% more volume without hiring"
- "Cut 10 hours/week of manual ops"
- "A system that runs on weekends without you"

**73% of buyers in 2025 prefer outcome-tied pricing** over time-based billing.

The strongest positioning: **specific pain + specific niche + proof of results.**
