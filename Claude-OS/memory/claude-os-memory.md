## Key Decisions Made
- **Name candidates:** ClarisOS (leading), Operator, Clarity OS, Claude Stack
- **Business model:** 3-tier (self-serve $97–$197 / DWY $500–$1,500 / DFY $2,500+ setup + $500/mo retainer)
- **Primary acquisition:** LinkedIn (existing 5,945 followers) → Reddit (secondary)
- **Direct competitor:** getclaudeos.com ($49 DIY, no support — we go premium + human)
- **The gap we fill:** Done-with-you, personalized, with proof. Nobody owns this at $500–$2,500.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
Solopreneur or 1–3 person team. $5K–$30K/month revenue. Has tried + abandoned 3+ tools. Works 50+ hours/week, still behind. "ADHD-ish." Age 28–42. LinkedIn/Reddit active. Wants something that actually works.

## Competitor Snapshot
- getclaudeos.com — $49, DIY, 33 modes + 158 skill files. No setup help.
- Notion OS (August Bradley) — $249, abandoned within weeks by most buyers
- Enterprise AI consulting — $15K–$35K, not for solopreneurs
- Gap: $500–$2,500 done-with-you with real proof

## Pricing Reference
- Initial audit/strategy: $1,500–$5,000 (market rate)
- Full setup: $5K–$15K (SMB market rate — our entry is below this)
- Monthly retainer: $1K–$3K (light), $3K–$10K (full)
- Our positioning: accessible premium ($500–$2,500 setup), with proof
